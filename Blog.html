I joined Ecobees as a web development intern in 2025. It’s a great opportunity to learn and explore how companies work in real environments. The team is friendly and supportive, which makes the experience more valuable.

My internship at Ecobees has just begun, but I am excited to grow and contribute as I learn more. I look forward to getting involved in upcoming tasks and gaining more experience.
